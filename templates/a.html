About Therapy Sessions

If you're experiencing depression, therapy can be an essential part of your treatment plan. Therapy sessions provide a safe and supportive environment to explore your feelings, thoughts, and experiences.

During therapy sessions, you'll work with a trained therapist who can help you develop coping strategies, challenge negative thought patterns, and build resilience.

Getting Help

If you're struggling with depression, it's essential to reach out for help. You can:

  • Talk to your doctor or a mental health professional.
  • Seek therapy from a licensed therapist or counselor.
  • Reach out to a trusted friend or family member for support.
  • Contact a mental health helpline or crisis hotline for immediate assistance.
