About Us

Our project, Depression Detection, aims to provide a solution for detecting and analyzing depression through audio and video inputs. We are a team of passionate developers and mental health advocates dedicated to leveraging technology to address mental health challenges.

Our mission is to create a platform that not only detects signs of depression but also provides support and resources for individuals struggling with mental health issues. Through our innovative approach, we aim to raise awareness, reduce stigma, and promote mental well-being.
